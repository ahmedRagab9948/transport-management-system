# Business Rules

- A trailer consists of:
  - Truck Head
  - Trailer

- Jumbo trucks have only one plate number.

- A driver can only be assigned to one active vehicle at a time.

- Trips can have the following statuses:
  - Pending
  - Assigned
  - In Progress
  - Completed
  - Cancelled

- Only admins can delete trips.

- OTP verification is required during login.
